Who We Are

A Legacy of Flavor
and Dedication

Maitree Foods was born from a deep love for food and community. Founded by Sachin Sonawane in Tingrenagar, Pune, what began as a humble catering venture has grown into one of Pune's trusted names in premium vegetarian event catering.

The name "Maitree" — meaning friendship and harmony in Sanskrit — reflects the spirit at the heart of everything we do. We believe that food is not merely sustenance; it is the thread that weaves people together at life's most precious moments.

Every event we cater is treated with the same care and devotion as cooking for family. From sourcing the finest fresh ingredients to the final presentation on the table, our entire team is dedicated to ensuring that your guests leave with full plates and warm memories.

Our Values

The Principles We Cook By

These are the commitments that guide every dish we prepare and every event we serve.

Unwavering Quality

We never compromise on the quality of ingredients. Every vegetable, every spice, every element is chosen with care to ensure the finest taste on your plate.

Hygiene First

Our kitchen and service operations adhere to the highest standards of cleanliness and food safety — because your health matters as much as your satisfaction.

Pure Vegetarian

Our kitchen is 100% vegetarian. We are proud to serve pure sattvic food that respects every dietary preference, tradition, and religious requirement.

Guest-First Approach

Your guests are our guests. We take pride in ensuring every person at your event is served with warmth, promptness, and genuine hospitality.

Tailored Menus

No two events are the same. We work closely with you to craft a menu that reflects your taste, tradition, and the spirit of your celebration.

Punctuality and Reliability

Events run on schedules. Our team arrives on time, sets up efficiently, and ensures seamless service throughout — every single time.
